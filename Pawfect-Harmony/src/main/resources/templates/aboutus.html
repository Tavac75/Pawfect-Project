This is the about us page

    Since 2023, Pawfect Harmony has worked to facilitate the adoption of animals through our peer-to-peer environment. We do this through several ways.
  • Owners can build a profile of the pet they want to place up for adoption.
  • Potential adopters have the ability to search for adoptable pets in their area and request to meet or adopt a pet.
  • A user account page allows for easy profile updates and contains shortcuts to pets that they have put up for adoption/would like to meet.
